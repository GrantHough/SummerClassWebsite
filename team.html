Grant Hough

Lead Instructor

Grant Hough is a rising senior at Saratoga High School with interests in computer science and percussion. He has received multiple computer science-related awards including 2nd place in the Congressional App Challenge and multiple hackathons. Grant is the primary instructor for "Zero to AI Web Apps" and is the designer of both the course curriculum and website. He's excited for this summer and hopes to bring the future of web apps within reach.
